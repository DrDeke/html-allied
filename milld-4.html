Mill D - Paper Machine Room

This big white pipe with the bend in it heads back towards the machine room, and pipes are always fun, so let's follow it!

 

On the way back to the machine room, one finds the upstairs portion of the freight elevator we saw in the basement. It has a sign on it saying something about it not being safe to ride, or something of that nature.

 

On a wall on the way to the machine room, somebody has decided to advocate doing what we were doing at the time.

 


This large bin (which leads to a big tank under the floor) is labeled 'Broke Pulper'. Rather than indicating that it is a pulper which is broken, 'Broke' is a term for paper that is in some way defective (there was a problem on the machine during manufacturing, somethng happened to the roll after it was made, the roll is of the wrong grade, etc...). Such paper would be loaded into the Broke Pulper to be turned back into pulp and re-made into new paper.

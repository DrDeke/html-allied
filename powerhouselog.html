Powerhouse Logbook from 1987

A logbook detailing the operation of the Powerhouse during May, June and July of 1987 was found in an upstairs office and has been reproduced photographically for our reading enjoyment.

For easier reading, I have transcribed the logbook pages into text. The page image may be viewed by clicking on the link for the date in question. Some of the text is difficult to read and some portions of the pages have been smudged or obliterated by being stepped on with dirty shoes. Unknown words or partial words are noted by dashes (---), unsure words and letters are noted by (?).

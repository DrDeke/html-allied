Allied Paper Mill of Kalamazoo Michigan:

Built in the 1890s, abandoned in 1998, the Allied Paper Mill now stands forlorn and empty. Much of the vast Allied Paper complex is gone, but the remaining buildings include Mills C and D, which once contained a total of 5 paper machines, the mill's Powerhouse, in which four enormous boilers still stand, and the partial ruins of Mill E, a former coating outfit.
